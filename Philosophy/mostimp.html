The Most Important Thing in Life

By Shubham Gautam

You know, for thousands and thousands of years Sun and Moon have shared the same sky, following each other around the world, merging till eternity. And, they’ll continue to do so…

But, have you ever realized the fact that Sun has it’s own light. It is capable of lighting the whole world, and that’s why it is known as “Luminous”, on the contrary Moon reflects the light of the sun to glow at night and thus is called “Non-luminous”?

For those many years, moon has always shared the light from sun and this is what makes moon dependent on sun. Yes! Moon is dependent on sun. Moon isn’t complete on itself, it always longs for sun and it’s light. Moon doesn’t have it’s own identity. Moon is beautiful, but have you ever thought what will happen the day when sun stops sharing it’s light to the moon? Will it be as beautiful as it is now? Will it be able to shine on it’s own? Will you be able to appreciate that blunt and dark circular ball in the sky? I think the answer to all the above questions is “No”! Now, what if moon leaves the side of sun? Will it cause any difference? Will the sun stop shining? Again, the answers are “No”.

So, coming to the question, what’s the most important thing in life?

Then, the most important thing in life is “You”. Yes! You are important. All that money, luxury and people may leave your side one day. But, if you’re complete on your own just like the sun is, you’ll sustain! Be the sun and emit your own light unlike moon seeking all the other things out of you and your control to complete you.

Good day!!
