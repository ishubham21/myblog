Letter-2: Unexpected

By Shubham Gautam

Dear Sara,

I must start this letter by saying "Sorry Sara". "Sorry I read your letter". Later last night, while on my workplace, I found your letter on my desk, maybe it was wrongly addressed to me. I had no plans of reading it, but the envelope was crumbled at places as if someone who wrote that was more caught with pains than thoughts in that letter as if someone was in tears. I'm hopeful and ashamed both. Hopeful for you, 'cause maybe your unaddressed letter is still addressed. Ashamed of me, 'cause, I read a letter which was meant to be private.

I know it wasn't for me, and I shouldn't be giving you hopes by replying to it. But that letter of yours was a piece of boundless adoration, and the faith of love and I wasn't able to resist the temptation of writing back. I don't know if it will ever reach you or you'll ever heed this, but I feel everyone deserves to be heard, everyone deserves to know a way out. I've been there, I've felt the same pains, but you know what? They faded. Yes! They eventually faded.

Reading your letter was heartbreaking, I was stuck with some deep realization as to "Is this love?". I know Sara that Rhean is the father of your unborn, which instantly sparks your feelings for him. But, ask your heart, is he actually a good person? Is your love worth waiting for someone who just took advantage of your body? Doesn't he deserve punishment? Ask yourself that had he taken your permission, would you, Sara had allowed him to use yourself? Ask your heart. It has all the answers.

You actually love Sara, but not that person. What you love are actually your child and the idea of falling in love with Rhean. This is not love dear!

Love is when you fall for someone's personality, love is when you love talking to someone, love is when you share a common heart, and love is when it is pure!

I know different people have different notions for love, you may not agree with mine. But you know what we all have common in our definitions of love?

It is Respect and Care. If you love someone, you respect them as an individual, for who they are, you care for them, in every state of your mind. Had Rhean respected you, he would never ever had forced himself onto you. Had he cared for you, he would've found you and your child out, but, did he? I know it can be hard for you to accept, but the reality stands clear in front of you.

Also, I want you to know that you can write me back anytime. You can trust me after all, broken people don't cheat.

Don't worry, time will bring you new hopes. Maybe not today, maybe not tomorrow, maybe not even a week from now, but eventually, it will.

Your secret confidant
Nazm
