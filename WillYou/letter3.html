Letter-3: Unexplainable

By Shubham Gautam

Hi Nazm, this is Sara...

I don't know what to say, you have already read everything my life has to offer.and please don't be sorry about reading that, I'm the one at fault, I was the jerk sending my life in cursive to Kashmir that too unaddressed.

I knew, I justifiably knew at the bottom of my heart that, that letter doesn't make any sense, I was a bit apprehensive and had crushed the letter several times, I was in a storm that not only drained my emotional self but also my ability to act and thus, I was allured at the idea "What if Rhean gets to read this? What if he comes back to me? What if this baby of ours gets a father's name?". But maybe god have some other plans for me.

I have read your letter a hundred times to understand what exactly my love for rhean is or if it's even love... probably you are right or a maybe!

I don't know but one day I will know, what it is...is it because of my loneliness or I am thinking of my child before myself or I don't know what it feels to be loved and wanted. Probably I will end up breaking my heart because I gave it to the wrong person, I have wasted a major of my prayers on someone who doesn't care about my existence or I am just attached to a idea of loving someone whom I wish he was. I am amidst a confusion and my heart (small laugh) has never been of much help to me!...it doesn't even know, whether to forget, forgive or wait.

But still... my love for him feels right, a different kind of right, I can't help but think of a parallel universe where we would wake up next to each other every morning or at night when he would kiss my forehead before tucking me to bed. I know I can't have one, so I have imagined one. This feeling makes me content, and has given me a purpose of life, please don't take let me believe otherwise...I have saved my heart, whole of my life waiting for the right person to win it, if he is not my right person now then I will have to wait longer for someone who will love me a little more than he does in "my world".

I still muse over his last words, "promise me, you won't"....and you know Nazm, the first thing which I did was breaking the only promise we had, the only...

I knew, that people who make ask for promises have been hurt a lot and need reassurance, and still...aaah. Maybe he had his own reasons for what he did to me, Maybe Rhean's side of story is unheard. Just maybe.

I am grateful to you for being so thoughtful about the different love I am keen on practicing, Nazm... You talked about pain and how it eventually faded. I feel you as an another mate who has suffered the different edge of love. Can I get to hear your side of your story? You too need to be heard, felt and healed. I hope you can confide in me. I am great listener Nazm, I can!

Waiting for your reply and story!

With care and a smile

A new friend
Sara
