Letter-14: Will You?

By Shubham Gautam

Dear Nazm,

I don't know what to say to that mature yet cutest proposal. I want to say a yes because I too have begun felling for you. It feels good to be loved, but this feeling is scaring the shit out of me. My earlier experiences had never been good and yet again, I fell for a wrong person, Rhean. I fear I am kind of afraid to get attached, to love, and then get ignored and broken. It is a vicious cycle, and I don't want to be a part of it. Yet I am craving for you, it would be a privilege to get my heart broken by you. The strangest thing is that I haven't actually met you and yet my love for you is this, I can't imagine what it would be like if we meet, we'll talk and I'll get lost in your eyes while you'll go on talking about life and the things I didn't knew.

You are the right person for me but I am not for you because you are perfect. We can't be together Nazm since I still don't know what I want from my life, from me and the people around me. I am still looking for my lost pieces. I can't take those pieces from you; I have to search mine. You can love someone only when you are complete yourself.

I am not certain about a lot of many things Nazm, but I am; for what I feel for you. And at the same time, I am afraid to fuck up. I feel as if everything I touch becomes trash. Everything! I don't want to screw things up with you, you deserve everything, you deserve to grow Nazm, you deserve to live for yourself.

I want to be right for you; all I am asking is some time, for I have some answers to seek.

Until then write to me regularly, your handwriting tells me a lot about you, the leaning I's, the curled u's and those beautiful s's. I actually have imagined your image in, tall, dusky and shy. I read all your letters in an imaginary masculine voice. And I know you too have an image for me.

I am just waiting for the right time, the time where I will be good enough for you too. But remember one thing; true love stories are those which are incomplete, for incompleteness holds on to hope until the very end. Yet, I wish it to last a forever with you.

But right now, I will say a no, I can't. I will one day come to you all by myself, but this is not the right time yet.

But you will wait for me, na? You will always remember me, will you Nazm?

With an aching heart

Truly yours
Sara
